Key Assumptions
Political Stability

Government policies remain supportive of NGO operations and childcare initiatives

Funding Continuity

Donor funding and grants continue to be available throughout project duration

Community Support

Local community remains engaged and supportive of the orphanage activities

Staff Retention

Key staff members remain with the organization, ensuring program continuity

Risks & Mitigation
Funding Cuts

Risk: Donor withdrawal or funding reduction
Mitigation: Diversify funding sources, develop income-generating activities

Disease Outbreak

Risk: Health crisis affecting children and staff
Mitigation: Implement strict health protocols, maintain emergency health fund

Natural Disaster

Risk: Floods or other natural events damaging infrastructure
Mitigation: Develop disaster preparedness plan, secure insurance coverage

Staff Turnover

Risk: Loss of key personnel
Mitigation: Competitive compensation, cross-training, succession planning
